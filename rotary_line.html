Carleton University Professor Gone Missing
The Charlatan — Campus News — 3 Days Ago

History professor Thomas Ellsworth has not been seen on campus since Tuesday morning, when colleagues report he left a faculty meeting abruptly and did not return to his office. Campus security confirmed his keycard was last used at the Dunton Tower faculty entrance at 9:14 AM.

Ellsworth was known for his research into the pre-development history of the land beneath Carleton's main campus, and had grown increasingly agitated in recent weeks. A colleague described him as "convinced he'd found something certain people did not want found."

His office in Dunton Tower has been sealed pending review. Students report he kept a personal locker in the Mackenzie Building — Locker 114 — and stored research materials there outside of his office.
